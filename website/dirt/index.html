DICOM Region Trender (DIRT)

DICOM Region Trender (DIRT) is a free software tool designed to analyze and trend regions in DICOM (Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine) files. It provides advanced features for medical image processing and analysis, making it a powerful resource for professionals and researchers. It is in Alpha stage and HAS HAD LIMITED TESING.

NOT FOR MEDICAL USE!! RESEARCH ONLY!!
